Welcome to the Subscription Manager App!


This is a simple application written in Python based on the Django and Bootstrap Frameworks.
It allows the user to easily manage all his subscriptions (recurring and non-recurring) to various services.
You no longer have to remember all your active subscriptions and worry about forgeting to renew them.
The App remebers all the necessary info about your subscription and displays the summary in a readable way.
You can also add custom reviews to your subscriptions and the App can then suggest you which subscription to continue and which to cancel.
It also allows the user to create custom Plans to easily group all your subscriptions
